Scholarship Policy for Session 2019

PGDM (2019-21)

To improve academic quality and inculcate urge for excellence, the scholarships are offered to PGDM Program students on the basis of merit.

I. Scholarship on the basis of Qualifying Exams

In our constant endeavor to encourage qualitative students, below are the qualifying exams score to determine scholarship:

S.No Marks in qualifying Exam Scholarship for the 1st Year
A CAT Score >80 percentile Rs. 50,000
CAT Score between 70-80 percentile Rs. 25,000
B CMAT Score > 80 percentile Rs. 50,000
CMAT Score Between 70 & 80 percentile Rs. 25,000
C MAT Score > 90 percentile Rs. 50,000
MAT Score between 80 & 90 percentile 25,000
II. Scholarship on the basis of Academic Background

Eligible students from any of the below mentioned graduation courses would be receiving scholarship only for First Year of PGDM Program as per below mentioned criterion:

A) B. Tech/BE/B.Arch

  • Marks > 70% throughout Academics (10th, 12th & Graduation) - Rs. 50,000
  • Marks 65% - 70% throughout Academics (10th, 12th & Graduation) - Rs. 25,000

B) Chartered Accountant/ Cost Accountants/ Company Secretary

  • Marks > 70% throughout Academics (10th,12th & Graduation) - Rs. 50,000
  • Marks 60% - 70% throughout Academics (10th,12th & Graduation) - Rs. 25,000

C) Other Degree Courses

  • Marks > 75% throughout Academics (10th,12th & Graduation) - Rs. 50,000
  • Marks 70% -75% throughout Academics (10th,12th & Graduation) - Rs. 25,000
III. Special Scholarship for the students of sister group institutions

Students from IMS Engineering College & IMS UC Campus, Adhyatmik Nagar will be given a special scholarship of Rs. 25,000.

IV. Scholarship for candidates with Work Experience

The candidates with work experience will be awarded with the scholarship as follows:

A Between 1 to 3 Years Rs. 25,000
V. Defence Personnel dependent Scholarship
  • The candidates, belonging for defense family will be awarded with the scholarship of Rs. 25,000.
VI. Siblings Scholarship

Waiver of Rs. 50,000 of tuition fees yearly to be deducted from the last installment of academic fees of the respective year applicable for 2 years.

  • The scholarship will be available for real siblings only for PGDM only.
  • The scholarship will be given after the submission of affidavit by the parents with some legal document endorsing the sibling relationship along with photo id of parents.
  • For cousin siblings the scholarship amount will be Rs 25,000/- one time on submission of proof of same paternal/ maternal grandparents
Guidelines
  1. Scholarship on the basis of Academic background mentioned under Point II (A) & (C), and Special scholarship for the students of the respective universities mentioned under point IV, will be applicable for FULL TIME courses only. This will not be applicable for point II (B).
  2. If any student is eligible for more than one category of scholarship the maximum scholarship will Rs 75,000.
  3. These scholarships are only for the First Year of PGDM Program.

Note: All the above mentioned Scholarships will be applicable to the students who have secured more than 50% in the Qualifying Exam.
