Life @ IMS

Lecture Rooms

Well-furnished air-conditioned spacious classrooms and lecture halls gives the future managers and technocrats a focused arena to develop their personalities. The latest audio-visual aids and multimedia technology enables the faculty members to have interactive sessions of teaching, which adds confidence and enthusiasm among our students.

Computer Centre

IMS Ghaziabad has created the most advanced Computer Labs. All the computers are connected via local area network (LAN) using the Server family OS along with Fortinet Firewall 200B and internet information server 6.0. We have dedicated internet connectivity of 10 MBPS (on fiber optics). The hardware configuration of the computer center is:

Eight Servers including IBM Core2Quad Server, HCL Server (Windows Server 2003, ISA & Fedora) and other Intel Based Server. Labs are equipped with HP Lase jet network printers. For the security purpose we are using Fortigate Firewall 200-B with analyzer, which can cater up-to 1000 clients. Uninterrupted power supply is available in all labs through power backup. Students may utilize computer lab facility as per the rule displayed and in the consulting with lab in-charge on duty.

A total Application software like MS Office 2007,MS Project, Business Beacon, Prowess, SPSS 16.0, SQL Server are extensively used. The Institute provides facilities for Scanning & printing include high speed Laser Jet Network printers. The Campus Network is protected using Fortinet Firewall 200B. Internet and Network Security is provided by Kaspersky Security Center. An Academic Alliance with Microsoft for all software's products is also registered as an Educational Campus agreement.

Total 10 MBPS Internet lease line has been installed in IMS Hostel and network connectivity is provided through Wi-Fi. (Wireless Fidelity) devices by the Netgear brand network products. A Hardware firewall model 310B by the renowned name in security system FORTIGATE is also installed to release the bandwidth without Viruses, Trojan, Malware and Spyware for students.

The computer wide network optical fiber backbone and structured cabling provides intranet as well as internet connectivity between/to all computers with faculty, students, PGDM academic block, administration, MDP rooms and Hostels.

Assessment software for grading, course registration, faculty feedback, attendance in class, conducting online evaluations, etc. has also been installed on the network. This can be selectively accessed by authorized Sections of IMS community. An e-Learning On-Line module is being used by the Faculty to provide Learning Notes, exercises, assignments and conduct Quizzes.

Auditorium

A fully air conditioned auditorium has the capacity of seating 400 people at a time. It is equipped with latest audio-visual system which enhances the effect of conferences, seminars, symposiums and other cultural events.

Library

IMS Library is fully air conditioned and has a collection of over 26000 books related to management IT and allied subjects. The Library holds a rich collection of printed as well as electronic resources which include books, journals, databases, CDs/DVDs, e-journals, reports, case studies, conference proceeding, training manuals, etc. The Library with its modern collection of knowledge resources and innovative information services fills an essential role for the academic community in their intellectual pursuits. Books: The Library has a highly selective collection of over 26000 books in the area of management information technology and tourism related subjects. Journals: The Library subscribes to 200 print journals specific to the academic and research needs of the IMS community. Besides, there are over 240 e-journals available in full-text through the online databases subscribed by the library. E-resources: The electronic library includes databases such as CMIE's Prowess, Business Beacon and DELNET online journals, Emerald, ICFAI University press. Other Resources: The library also holds a collection of case studies, annual reports of companies, etc., in the area of management and related subjects.

Clubs @ IMS
Finance Club

Finance Club nurtures and enhances the fin quotient of the students and fosters an environment that encourages a culture of continuous learning and application of the same. The club has been formed with an objective of sharing knowledge and information about the financial sector and bringing about active participation. It is the most popular forum of knowledge sharing in finance, beyond the realms of classroom; it includes workshops, guest lectures, games, and quizzes.

The club organizes a number of events such as Business Quiz, Role Play Competition , Money Magic, IPO watch, Apna Sapna Money Money etc. In addition to these the students also organize "FINNACLE " - The Finance Fest.

Marketing Club (Markwizz)

The marketing Club of IMS was constituted with the objective to provide avenues to our budding managers for exploring and discovering their hidden potential. It is a platform for unleashing their creativity and innovation through organization of various competitive events based on current marketing scenario prevailing in India & world wide. Till date, various events like MarkT Vyom (Marketing Quiz Competition) MarkT Aaknti ( Bill-Board Designing Competition on the Theme "Protect Girl child "), AD-diction ( Unlimited Innovations) , MarkT Sangram (The Ultimate Marketing Enigma), MarkT Kala have been organized by this club. Marketing Club IMS also organizes an Annual Inter-Institute Marketing Festival, MarkT Fest which is a blend of diverse events. In these events, the students not only participate wholeheartedly but also learn the intricacies of marketing concepts, which enable them to hone their organizing and marketing skills.

Besides this, Marketing Club IMS runs a regular Wall Magazines, MarkT Zine, which displays current articles regarding the happenings in the field of Marketing & Advertising.

Saksham:The HR Club Evidence of excellence

In an endeavor to achieve excellence, Saksham the HR Club constantly organizes innovative and meaningful event, that delivers experiment based learning activities built around cquiring knowledge as well as having fun and adventure. Our HR fest is specially designed for preparing the students to achieve excellence in their professional careers. It organizes workshops, guest lectures, games, and quizzes.

Till date, various competitive events were organized by Saksham; the HR club. Some of them are Juggernauts, Whizkid, Synergims 2010, Aaron 2011. They comprised of many sub events like Creative Punch (to enhance and assess the creativity level of the students), Sangathan, Team it up (a competitive game to develop the team sprit), Abhiney (a situation based role play), theme based documentary film and many other events. Saksham also runs an annual e – magazine of HR, named; "COMPETENCE" which is completely handled by Core Committees and Editorial Committee of HR students under the guidance and supervision of faculty in charge. The students of different streams are encouraged to contribute in this magazine.

Aayam Operation Club

It is a platform for enthusiasts to nurture and enhance their theoretical foundations and application in the field of Strategy and Operations Management. This Operation Club of IMS Ghaziabad has been formed with an objective of sharing knowledge and information in the areas of Systems and Operations.

Members are updated on the latest trends and developments in these fields by regular club meetings. Key activities of the club include various strategic games related to Operations and Systems (Quizzes, SCM and Strategic Games etc.), Workshop , Guest lectures, Individual and Group Projects in the field of Strategy, system & operations and Industrial visits.

Fest @ IMS

Melange -17, An Inter Institute Annual Cultural Festival, March 24-25, 2017 at IMS Ghaziabad, Lal Kaun Campus

IMS Ghaziabad organized its Inter Institute Annual Cultural Festival “Melange -17”, March 24-25, 2017. The Mega Event was inaugurated by Ms. Nidhi Kesarwani, (IAS), Honorable District Magistrate, Ghaziabad in the august presence of Sh. Pramod Agarwal, Managing Trustee IMS Ghaziabad, Sh. Sanjay Agarwal, Chairman IMS Society, Sh. Rakesh Chharia, General Secretary IMS Society, Dr. Sraban Mukherjee, Director IMSEC and Director IMS Ghaziabad.

Director IMS Ghaziabad welcomed the Chief Guest & other dignitaries and congratulated the Faculty & Staff for organizing a huge and wonderful event. Ms. Nidhi kesarwani advised the students to stick to their roots, culture, respect thy country and urged them to be patient, to work hard and most of all be a good human being. The Event flagged off by spectacular performances from students and witnessed huge participation from around 70 reputed Universities / Colleges including 17 Teams from Delhi University Departments & Colleges in various events. At the end, all the participants, Faculty, staff and students were thanked by the conveners - Dr. Tapan kumar Nayak, Programme Chairperson - PGDM and Dr. Avadhesh Kumar Gupta, Head MCA
