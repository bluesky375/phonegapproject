PGDM-Big Data Analytics Admission Procedure

The procedure for PGDM-Big Data Analytics Two year Full-Time is elucidated for your reference.

  • Recognition/Accreditation/Approval: AICTE
  • Intake: 60
  • Eligibility Criteria: Graduation (minimum 50% marks.)
  • Selection Criteria: CAT/MAT/CMAT/XAT + GD-PI + Written Test
  • Tuition fee (for full course): INR 7,80,000.00
Eligibility Criteria for Admission to PGDM-Big Data Analytics
  1. A Valid score of MAT/CAT/CMAT/NMAT/XAT
  2. Passed Bachelor’s degree of minimum 3 – years of duration
    Obtained at least 50% marks (45% marks in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the qualifying Examination.

NOTE: It is desired that aspirants complete all academic requirements before joining the program to enable them to keep pace with the rigor and internal assessment schedule of the program.

Final Selection Criteria

Admission to PGDM-Big Data Analytics Programme is selective and based on a combination of factors including academic and professional achievement, performance on a standardized test and strong communication skills. The merit is prepared on the basis of parameters given below:

  • Personal Interview: 25%
  • Group Discussion: 25%
  • Written Expression Test: 10%
  • CAT/MAT/CMAT Score: 20%
  • Grad % + 12th % + 10th %: 20%

Admissions are granted to those applicants who have fulfilled all the requirements and whose name appears in the regular merit list of successful applicants.

*Note: Applying for Admission does not ensure that the candidate will be called for GD-PI process or get admission. The discretion of the admission committee of the Institute will be final in all such matters

How to Apply

Please follow the steps for selection in PGDM-Big Data Analytics Programme at IMS Ghaziabad

Step I
Procurement of Form

Application form can be acquired from 1st October 2018 onwards.

  1. Application form can also be filled on-line at our website by paying an amount of Rs. 1100/-
  2. Application form can be downloaded from the Website, filled by the candidate and sent to IMS along with a D.D. of Rs. 1100/- in favor of IMS Ghaziabad, payable at Delhi.
  3. Application form can be purchased from the counter at IMS Ghaziabad by paying Rs. 1100/-
  4. Application form can be obtained by sending a D.D. of Rs. 1150/- in favor of IMS Ghaziabad, payable at Delhi.
List of Documents Required

A candidate has to apply for admission to IMS Ghaziabad and attach duly attested copies of the following documents:

  1. Attested copy of CAT/ MAT/ GMAT/CMAT/ATMA score.
  2. Certificate and Mark Sheet of qualifying examination passed.
  3. Relevant certificate to verify date of birth.

Step II
Submission of Form

Offline: Applicant need to send the duly filled in form to IMS Ghaziabad, i.e. Institute of Management Studies, Lal Quan, GT Road, Ghaziabad – 201009 Uttar Pradesh (NCR).

Online: Applicants can submit the application form online at http://54.169.142.172

Step III
Call for GD-PI

Venue & Time Schedule shall be available on our website (URL: https://ims-ghaziabad.ac.in/admission/gd-pi-centres)

Step IV
Declaration of Final Selection List

List of the shortlisted candidates will be declared within 3 working days from the date of GD-PI held.

Step V
Fees Submission

A period of one month would be given, to the selected candidates from the day of declaration of the result to submit the first installment.

Academic session of all the programmes shall commence from July, 2018 (tentative).
