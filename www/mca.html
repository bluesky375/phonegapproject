MCA (Masters of Computer Applications)

(APJAKTU College Code - 036)

Master of Computer Application (MCA) at IMS Ghaziabad (affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. AKTU Lucknow and approved by AICTE) is one of the best courses in the entire North India and Delhi NCR offering excellent placements in Top MNC and Global I.T organizations from the last 29 years. MCA (3 Year Programme) is exclusively designed with a lot of emphasis on planning, designing and developing of complex commercial application software & system software to meet the growing demand of any organization in the field of Information Technology.

At IMS Ghaziabad, all efforts are directed towards developing the Technical, Analytical, person skills so that they can match up to the competition and become a well cultured person with all the multi-tasking abilities, required to be SMART TECHNOCRATS.

The programme is designed to provide the necessary theoretical background and practical exposure in Computer Science and Applications, to meet the growing manpower requirements of the Global I.T / Software Industry. After completion of MCA, Students have excellent job opportunities to enter the Global IT-world as well as the fields of Research & Development at prestigious institutions in India and abroad at different positions viz. Software Engineers, Testers, ERP Consultants, System Manager, Web Designers, Database Administrator, System Analyst, Business Analyst, Network Administrator, Entrepreneurship, Software Developer etc.
