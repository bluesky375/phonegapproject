Post Graduate Diploma in Management - Big Data Analytics (PGDM-Big Data Analytics)

About PGDM Big Data Analytics

The comprehensive curriculum of this program provides a framework which enables participants to gain in-depth and hands-on knowledge of Analytics and Big Data Concepts. All students will get an opportunity to expand their knowledge of Big Data Analytics and gain a strategic perspective of the industry. We enable our learners to meet the ever changing demands of the data driven world by giving them access and exposure to world class faculty along with mentorship from industry experts. International Data Corp. (IDC) forecasts that data production will increase to 40 Zettabytes (or 40 billion terabytes) by 2020, 50 times more than in 2010. This enormous amount of data can be used by business enterprises to generate new insights, enable better decision making and improve processes of organizations. Business analytics refers to the analysis of data using statistical, machine learning and quantitative techniques with the purpose of understanding past performance of the business and generating new insights for the future. The PGDM- Big Data Analytics to be offered by IMS-Ghaziabad, aims to help shape the emerging profession of business analytics by delivering a cutting edge inter disciplinary educational experience to graduate applicants with an aspiration of building a career in this field.

Program Objectives

This is a two year full time programme aimed at creating business analytics professionals employable by leading Indian and foreign firms. This programme is designed to develop the analytical mindset of the students enrolled and generate interest in tackling challenging business problems, and possess an inclination towards analytics. Some of the salient features of the programme will include courses taught by reputed faculty members at the campus, hands-on business analytics and Big Data training, internship project in renowned industry, continuous interaction with industry leaders throughout the course of the programme, and the availability of excellent placement opportunities in the industry.

Expected Outcomes

The teaching-learning cycle of this highly interactive Program in Business Analytics and Big Data involves a judicious mix of a wide range of pedagogical approaches:

  • Apply data and analytics to reshape business models, operations, and decision-making processes
  • Gain a fundamental understanding of big data and learn how to apply data perspectives for competitive advantage
  • Explore how experimentation and predictive analysis can improve the business model and operations of a firm
  • Integrate big data into marketing, supply chains, human resources, and other key functions
  • Recognize big data as a valuable resource for building and implementing strategic initiatives
  • Build and sustain a competitive advantage by expanding a company's analytics capabilities
Pedagogy

The teaching-learning cycle of this highly interactive Program in Business Analytics and Big Data involves a judicious mix of a wide range of pedagogical approaches:

  • Classroom lectures by leading industry professionals and distinguished faculty from the Subject Domain
  • On hand workshop/laboratory practice of the Analytical tools & Techniques
  • Case studies/Simulations/Real Data from the industry
  • Industry research project
  • Domestic and international exposure in the area of Business Analytics and Big Data Computing
  • Developing and implementing business models for informed decision-making
  • Frequent interaction with leading industry practitioners and data scientists
  • Brainstorming Sessions and Peer-to-Peer Learning
  • Management Games and outdoor Activities
Industry Engagement

The Program is designed with meticulous inputs and insights from leading industry practitioners regarding program curriculum, case studies, analytical methodologies, data sets, business problems and analytics projects to align the Program to the industry requirements.

A key highlight of the Program is the "Industry Analytics Project", fostering solid industry-academia partnership. Through the Project, students would need to identify a dataset, business problem and do a comprehensive analysis that results in a detailed report and presentation made that highlights the findings, methodology and implications for business.
